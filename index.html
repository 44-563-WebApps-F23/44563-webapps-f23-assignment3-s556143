Beach

The beach is where I like to vacation most.I adore listening to the sound of the ocean lapping at the shore, feeling the sand under my toes, and seeing a stunning sunset over the horizon.Being close to the ocean has a relaxing effect that helps me unwind and feel at peace with myself. There are lots of activities available, such as swimming in clean waters or making sandcastles with loved ones.
